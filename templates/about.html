About

Siri Beauty has been cutting and coloring the hair of Hyderabad City for over 15 years. Our talented team cut their teeth working backstage at New York Fashion Week runways and in the pages of magazines, but they also take pride in keeping a finger on the pulse of what makes every neighborhood unique. It’s that blend of experience and skill that makes New York City look like New York City. We thrive off the energy of this city and the unique people who call it home.

We keep our vision and our shears sharpened because we know as well as our clients do that trends come and go but hair always grows—and so should what your salon can offer you.
